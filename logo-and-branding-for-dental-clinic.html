Logo and branding for dental clinic

A fresh and welcoming visual identity that combines professionalism with approachability.

This logo and branding were created for a modern dental clinic SmilePoint, aiming to make patients feel comfortable and confident in their care.

The design features clean lines, soft colors, and friendly typography to convey trust, hygiene, and a caring atmosphere.
The icon is formed by letters that create a smile, symbolizing hospitality and warmth, while the overall style balances clinical precision with a human touch.
The logo conveys an association with an impeccable smile, the friendliness of the staff, and a commitment to innovation.
