Logo and identity for the Dental Conference

The Pediatric Dentists Conference is held twice a year, during summer and winter, each with its own unique theme. 
For each conference, a corporate style was developed to match the theme and color scheme of the logo. This style extended to various elements, including badges, photo zones, advertising banners, and souvenirs, all designed to create a cohesive and visually appealing experience.

The design reflects this by featuring a logo with a consistent frame, while the name, color scheme, and thematic symbols inside the logo are customized for each conference to align with the specific topic.
